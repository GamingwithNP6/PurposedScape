Our Story

We Know the
Desert.

PurposedScape was founded in 2019 by Marco Delgado, a lifelong Albuquerque native with a deep love for the high desert landscape. After years watching homeowners fight against the climate instead of working with it, Marco set out to build a landscaping company rooted in intentionality — using native plants, smart water systems, and timeless design to create outdoor spaces that thrive in New Mexico.

Today, PurposedScape serves Albuquerque, Rio Rancho, Corrales, and Bernalillo — bringing desert-smart design and old-fashioned craftsmanship to every project, big or small.
